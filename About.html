About SixIron Customs

Growing up in a small farming community in Texas, I have learned that hard work is a way of life. My father taught me the importance of being able to fix anything that is broken yourself and helped me learn many different trades.
What began as building something out of necessity, turned into a love for creating custom wood furniture. In today's throw away culture, I think it is still important to have furniture that will last a lifetime.
Everyone can have a one of a kind piece of furniture that starts as a piece of art in their home and turns into a family heirloom.
Contact me to learn more about how I can work with you and create your next piece of six iron custom furniture.


Our furniture pieces are:

  • made of high grade timber and each piece is made by hand
  • made with historical joinery to produce pieces that will be passed down for generations
